Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Remy app ("App").

Personal Information We Collect

When you use the App, we collect certain information about your device, including information about your reminders, notification preferences, app usage patterns, and device information. We refer to this automatically-collected information as "Device Information."

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • The App stores information locally on your device to provide the reminder functionality.
  • Diagnostic information is collected if you enable it, such as crash reports and performance data.
  • Usage analytics may be collected to improve the App's functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the Device Information that we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the App functionality;
  • Send notifications based on your reminder settings;
  • Troubleshoot and debug technical issues;
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience;
  • Communicate with you about updates and new features.

Data Storage and Security

Your reminder data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud backup, this data is securely stored with industry-standard encryption practices. We implement appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not share your Personal Information with third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy. We may share your Personal Information with service providers who assist us in operating the App, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Your Rights

You have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. You can do this through the App settings or by contacting us directly.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at: cingiz167@icloud.com.
